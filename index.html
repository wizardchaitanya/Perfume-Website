Our Story

Born from the art of patience

AURA began in a small atelier nestled in the hills of Grasse — the perfume capital of the world. Founded by master perfumer Élise Morin, each fragrance is an intimate dialogue between rare botanicals, aged woods, and time itself.

We believe a scent is more than a product — it is a memory, an emotion, a second skin. Every bottle that leaves our atelier carries six months of meticulous creation, distilled into something that endures.
